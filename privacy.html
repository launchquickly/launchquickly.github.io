Privacy

You can contact our Data Protection Office about this privacy policy at:

  • email: hello@launchquickly.scot, or
  • write to: Data Protection Officer, Launchquickly Limited, 32 Darley Crescent, Troon KA10 6JH

Policy overview

Launchquickly Limited looks to provide organisations with the means to create and manage successful online services themselves.

Our registered address is 32 Darley Crescent, Troon KA10 6JH and our registered company number is SC657639.

This privacy policy will explain how our organisation uses the personal data we collect from you when you use this website.

Personal data that we collect

Contact data

  • email address — when you communicate through email
  • contact data — when shared with us, may include your address, email address and telephone numbers

Other data

  • your Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • details of which web browser you used

Analytics and tracking data
We also use Google Analytics to collect anonymised information about:

  • which type of browser and device you used
  • the pages you visit on launchquickly.scot
  • how long you spend on each page
  • how you got to the site
  • what you click on while you’re visiting the site

Google Analytics data is only collected from users who have consented to share this data. Any information we get about you from Google Analytics will be anonymised before we see it.

We do not store your personal information through Google Analytics (for example your name or address).

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide us with the data we collect by contacting us. If you have consented anonymised data is also collected by Google Analytics.

How will we use your data?

We collect your data so that we can:

  • respond to your queries
  • inform you of news or offers relating to the services and products we provide, if you have registered an interest
  • to improve our services

We will not:

  • sell or give your data to third parties
  • share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

How do we store your data?

We use a third-party provider to store emails and documents you send us. Our third-party provider is G Suite. Their privacy policy is available here: G Suite privacy policy

Data collected by Google Analytics data may be processed outside the EEA. Their privacy policy is available here: Google Analytics privacy policy

Marketing

We would like to send you information about products and services of ours that we think you might like. If you have agreed to receive marketing, you may always opt out at a later date.

If you no longer wish to be contacted for marketing purposes, please email hello@launchquickly.scot to be removed.

What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. You have the right to:

  • see your information, or request a copy
  • improve your data – if you think the information, we have is inaccurate or incomplete
  • delete your information, — if you do not want us to use your data anymore
  • limit what we can do with your data, under certain conditions
  • stop us using your data, under certain conditions
  • take your data with you, under certain conditions

If you are looking to request any of the above, please either:

  • email: hello@launchquickly.scot, or
  • write to: Privacy request, Launchquickly Limited, 32 Darley Crescent, Troon KA10 6JH

Privacy policies of other websites

Our website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

How to complain

If you are unhappy with how we have used your data, you can complain to the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO):

  • phone: 0303 123 1113, or
  • write to: Information Commissioner’s Office, Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, Cheshire SK9 5AF

Changes to this policy

We may periodically update this policy. This policy was last reviewed on 06 October 2020.

Questions
