Personal data that we collect

Contact data

  • email address — when you communicate through email

Analytics and tracking data

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide us with the data we collect by contacting us.

How will we use your data?

We collect your data so that we can:

  • respond to your queries
  • inform you of news or offers relating to the services and products we provide, if you have registered an interest

We will not:

  • sell or give your data to third parties
  • share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

How do we store your data?

We use a third-party provider to store emails and documents you send us. Our third-party provider is G Suite. Their privacy policy is available here: G Suite privacy policy

We do not store your data elsewhere.

Marketing

We would like to send you information about products and services of ours that we think you might like. If you have agreed to receive marketing, you may always opt out at a later date.

If you no longer wish to be contacted for marketing purposes, please email hello@launchquickly.scot to be removed.

What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. You have the right to:

  • see your information, or request a copy
  • improve your data – if you think the information, we have is inaccurate or incomplete
  • delete your information, — if you do not want us to use your data anymore
  • limit what we can do with your data, under certain conditions
  • stop us using your data, under certain conditions
  • take your data with you, under certain conditions

If you are looking to request any of the above, please either:

  • email: hello@launchquickly.scot, or
  • write to: Privacy request, Launchquickly Limited, 32 Darley Crescent, Troon KA10 6JH

Privacy policies of other websites

Our website contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.
